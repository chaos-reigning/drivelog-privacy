Support DriveLog

DriveLog is a passion project, built and maintained by one person in their spare time. There's no team, no investors, and no advertising. The app is free, your data stays on your device, and there's no plan to change either of those things.

If DriveLog has made your drives a little more memorable and you'd like to give back, there are a few ways to do it:

Tip the developer

You can send a one-time tip of any amount via Ko-fi. Tips help cover the Google Maps API costs, the Play Store developer fee, and the occasional cup of coffee while building the next feature.

☕ Tip on Ko-fi

Tips are completely voluntary and do not unlock any in-app features. The DriveLog Pro upgrade — themes, vehicle name, and the map thumbnail on the share card — is the only paid feature in the app and is purchased through Google Play Billing inside the app.

Free ways to help

  • Rate the app on Google Play. Honest reviews go a long way for indie apps.
  • Tell a friend who drives. Word-of-mouth is how DriveLog grows.
  • Send feedback. Found a bug, have an idea, or want a feature? Email donnielg3@gmail.com.
