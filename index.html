Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 6, 2025

DriveLog is a drive tracking app that records GPS data to calculate your speed, distance, and route while driving. This policy explains what data is collected, how it is used, and your rights regarding that data.

What we collect

While you are actively recording a drive, DriveLog collects:

  • GPS coordinates (latitude and longitude)
  • Speed at each recorded point
  • Timestamps

This data is collected only when you tap Start Drive and stops the moment you tap Finish. No data is collected at any other time.

How your data is stored

All trip data is stored exclusively on your device using local storage. It is never uploaded to any server, cloud service, or database operated by us. Your drives stay on your phone and nowhere else.

How your data is used

Collected location data is used solely to:

  • Calculate trip statistics (top speed, average speed, distance, duration)
  • Draw your route on the in-app map
  • Generate shareable trip cards at your request

We do not use your data for advertising, analytics, or any other purpose.

Data sharing

We do not sell, share, rent, or transmit your personal data to any third party. The only exception is if you choose to use the share feature, in which case you are sharing the content — we have no involvement in or access to that action.

Third-party services

DriveLog uses the Google Maps API to display route maps on share cards (Pro feature). When this feature is used, sampled GPS coordinates from your trip are sent to Google's servers to render a static map image. This is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

No other third-party SDKs, analytics tools, or advertising frameworks are present in the app.

Background location

DriveLog requests background location permission so that GPS tracking continues accurately if your screen turns off during a drive. This permission is used solely for active recording sessions and for no other purpose.

Data retention & deletion

Your trip data is stored on your device for as long as you choose to keep it. You can delete individual trips at any time from within the app by long-pressing a trip and selecting Delete. Uninstalling the app removes all data permanently.

Children's privacy

DriveLog is not directed at children under the age of 13 and we do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to this policy

If we make material changes to this privacy policy, we will update the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
